Sam and Matt's Spicy Peanut Pasta

Just fucking delicious spicy peanut butter vegetable delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 unit protein if wanted

  • 1 to 2 tbsp olive oil(depends if air fry protein/how oily)

  • 4 cups broth

  • 2 to 4 tbsp agave/maple syrup (depends how sweet)

  • 4 tbsp soy sauce

  • 1/2 to 1 cup Peanut Butter (how much nuts you want)

  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (can add more to thicken)

  • 2 tsp ground ginger (can also use fresh)

  • 2 tsp hot pepper flakes (more is good too if you want spicy spicy)

  • 1 tsp cayenne

  • 2 to 4 scoops garlic

  • As much vegetables as you want

    • Broccoli

    • Green Onion

    • Green Bell Pepper

    • Zuccini

    • Literally anything
