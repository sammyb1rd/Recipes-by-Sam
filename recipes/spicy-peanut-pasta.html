Sam and Matt's Spicy Peanut Pasta

Just delicious spicy peanut butter vegetable delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 thing of noodles

  • 1 unit protein if wanted

  • 1 to 2 tbsp olive oil(depends if air fry protein/how oily)

  • 4 cups broth

  • 2 to 4 tbsp agave/maple syrup (depends how sweet)

  • 4 tbsp soy sauce

  • 1/2 to 1 cup Peanut Butter (how much nuts you want)

  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (can add more to thicken)

  • 2 tsp ground ginger (can also use fresh)

  • 2 tsp hot pepper flakes (more is good too if you want spicy spicy)

  • 1 tsp cayenne

  • 2 to 4 scoops garlic

  • As much vegetables as you want

    • Broccoli

    • Green Onion

    • Green Bell Pepper

    • Zuccini

    • Literally anything

Instructions

  1. Heat up protein with optional oil in large skillet cook on heat heat for 5-7 minutes. Remove protein from pan.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together broth, agave, soy sauce, peanut butter, cornstarch, ginger, cayenne, and hot pepper flakes.

  3. Pour this mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until thickened and smooth.

  4. Add the green onions, garlic, and whatever vegetables you want, cook for another few minutes.

  5. Return protein to the pan and simmer the mixture for 2-3 minutes, until heated through.

  6. Serve over hot, cooked pasta or mix all together.

Thank you! Enjoy your food.
